Me, now a days

So, I used to be a deep thinker. Always wondering the why and how of things I couldn't comprehend. I used to wander through the city beating my mind with ideals, hopes and dreams never to come true; let's be honest here, that was worthless and really stressful, but it did teach me a lot about myself and others and I'm not ashamed to say I learned a lot from my own depression.

First, I learned nothing was given for free in life, you need to fight to the bitter end and never let go of the things you cherish, or they are gonna be taken away from you.

Second, I learned, over anything you can get for yourself, never hurt others to get it, for nothing is worth shit if you hurt others for it.

Third and most important of all, smile, be happy, don't you ever let anyone take away who you are, for most will try and if they succeed, life will be a hell. Don't you ever forget you are the one person who loves you the most, knows you the most, does anything for you and always forgives you, so you owe that person the very best you can give him or her. This... This is my new philosophy of life, and trust me, it is worthy.
